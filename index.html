About Glisten

Connecting Talent with Opportunity to Build Exceptional Teams.

Glisten Mind Providers is a fast-growing start-up founded in 2023 and based in Ahmedabad. We specialize in staffing, business consulting, technology services, and professional services, providing innovative turnkey solutions to tackle complex hiring challenges.
Our team of experienced HR and talent acquisition professionals leverages strong technological and managerial expertise to understand and meet our clients' resource needs. We focus on resolving hiring challenges for both product and service companies.
With a vision to streamline the end-to-end recruitment cycle, Glisten helps organizations reduce costs and make informed hiring decisions. Although newly established, we bring extensive knowledge of the staffing industry.
Our unique selling proposition is tailored recruitment services supported by deep expertise across multiple sectors. We prioritize professionalism in representing both our clients and candidates, collaborating with prominent companies and innovative tech start-ups.
